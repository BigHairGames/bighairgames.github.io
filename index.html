Planet VS Planet

About

Planet vs Planet (or, PvP for short) is a local multiplayer physics-based-planetary-arena-deathmatch (PBPAD) where players are tethered to one another with a giant, indestructible space rope.

Fling your opponent into planets, asteroids and other hazards celestial bodies and avoid annihalating yourself in the process. Collect cool powerups to help you survive the arena.

How To Play

Player 1: WASD

Player 2: Arrow Keys

You can also plug in your favorite generic gamepad Guide your rocket powered world to glory by steering it into powerups and avoiding planets
